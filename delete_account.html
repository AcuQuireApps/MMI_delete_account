How to Delete Your Account in MindMatIQ

To delete your account and all associated personal data:

  1. Open the MindMatIQ app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap on the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm your action to permanently delete your account and all related data.

Your data will be deleted from our servers within 7 days.
